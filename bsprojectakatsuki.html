About Team Akatsuki



Team Akatsuki

We are a group of friends and family that have one common hobby, which is cars. Team Akatsuki was established in 2018. Befor Team Akatsuki was created, we were just a regular big car group that love going to car shows and events in the Southeast region of the US. We would travel around North Carolina, Georgia, Tennessee, and Virginia. When we was doing all this, we were like, why not just name our group something since we all be going to these events as a whole already. So therefore, thats how Team Akatsuki is created.

Akatsuki's literal meaning is "dawn" or "daybreak." This is a group of shinobi from the anime show Naruto. Akatsuki is a group that seeks to make the world a better place through their own means. We called our self "Team Akatsuki" because we as car enthuisiasts believe we can make the car scene (compared to the world) better by having unique cars that can inspire a lot of people and show people that with hard work and dedication, anything is possible.

So below are some of the cars thats in our group. These cars are what made who we are and how our group is so close to each other that we consider each other as family. We are a group that wants to be different and prefer quality over quantity. We strive and challenge ourselves to not be a regualr car group but one that is "Unique as an individual and strong as a team."
